Who we are

Sanlayan is a Sanskrit word which means fusion , The very idea behind the formation of the organization is the fusion (Sanlayan) of every stake holder in the value chain for the well being of all the skate holder .We started as a proprietorship company during 2018 with an unique idea of using JLG model of micro lending in providing consumer appliances of income generation , clean energy , general household appliances and furnishing item to Rural/semi urban household to whom finance from formal main stream institution is not available .Having successfully tested the model, we scaled it up and incorporated as private limited company on during July 2019 as Sanlayan India Private Limited.
